Worldle is an interesting, challenging and fun word game. If you aren't familiar with it, I suggest that you play it before reading this article

Notice that there are a bunch of proper names? Let's look for only lowercase words as input and Uppercase words in the output of our query.

explore: words is table('malloy-data.malloytest.words'){
  query: five_letter_words is {
    where: length(word) = 5 and  word ~ r'^[a-z]{5}$'
    project: word is UPPER(word)
  }
}

Searching for Words

Regular expressions are great for matching words. We are going to use a few patterns.

This letter exists somewhere in the word

Find words that contain X AND Y.

query: words->five_letter_words->{
  where: word ~ r'[X]' & ~ r'[Y]'
  project: word
}
word
PROXY
SIXTY

Letter position: This letter here, that letter NOT there

Find words that have M in the 4th position and the 5th position is NOT E or Z

query: words->five_letter_words->{
  where: word ~ r'...M[^EZ]'
  project: word
